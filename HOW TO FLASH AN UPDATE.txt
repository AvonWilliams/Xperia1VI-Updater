    Can you please make a guide on how to update the phone with those tools?

Here is how I updated the phone:
1. Download Newflasher and the latest ROM via xperifirm
2. Extract the firmware and Newflasher.
3. Copy Newflasher files into the same folder where you extracted the ROM
4. In the ROM folder I deleted the file "persist_X-FLASH-ALL-88DF.sin". Not sure if it is needed, but I wanted to make sure the persist partition is not being flashed.
5. press and hold (power+ vol down) while plugging the usb cable. You should be seeing a green asterisk.
6. From within the ROM folder open a terminal window and type
Code:

newflasher set_active:a

and then just
Code:

newflasher

7. Follow the steps and when you are being asked to pull a dump select no, cause it doesn't work.
8. To re-gain root: Patch the init_boot.img with magisk and boot the phone into fastboot
9. Flash the image typing:
Code:

fastboot flash init_boot magisk_patched.img
